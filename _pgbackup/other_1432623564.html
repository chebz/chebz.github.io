37 Days To DIE

A top-down twin stick shooter for XBOX360. Developed using XNA by a team of 3 cool dudes. I was responsible for majority of programming, including AI, gameplay, fancy shaders, particle effects, boss behaviors, performance optimization, and leading by example! ;)

GoonyCru: Day One

A silly little game that involves customer service, grenades, and angry managers! I guess if you are stressed out about working in retail, then this is your thing. Propel customers with grenades around the screen, blow up angry managers, and feel like your IQ is dropping 10 points every minute you are playing this nonsense. ;)
I did the programming, another bored out of his mind dude did the art and game design.
